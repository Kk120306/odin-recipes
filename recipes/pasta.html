Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil

Directions

  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 minutes
  3. Drain the pasta and put it back in the pot
  4. Add olive oil, tomato sauce, parmesan cheese, and basil
  5. Mix well and serve hot
